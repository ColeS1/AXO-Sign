Functions that allow the LEDs to do 4 different combinations.

There is a button that will be pressed in order to go through each of the combinations.

1st - Stay on solid

2nd - Blink on for 1 seconds blink off for one second

3rd - Fade in and out (both red and green LEDs at the same time)

4th - Fade in and out (red fade in, green fade out, then green fade in, and red fade out.
